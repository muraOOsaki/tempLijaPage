BR[AI]N - MA Digital Media (Image-Making) Exhibition Film

This project was exhibited as part of the Mindscapes Degree Show: featuring work from MA Digital Media: Image Making, MA Digital Media: Data Visualisation, and MA Photography: The Image and Electronic Arts at Goldsmiths, University of London.

Film

Project Overview:

BR[AI]N explores the convergence of artificial intelligence and the human mind through 3D and 2D animation.The film delves into the intricate relationship between technology and human cognition, presenting a visual narrative that reflects on the impact of AI on our understanding of consciousness.

My Role & Tools:

Director, Designer & Animator | Adobe After Effects, Premiere Pro, Illustrator, Blender


Screened at the Stuart Hall Building, Goldsmiths University of London. MA Digital Media and Photography 'Mindscapes Exhibition'.
